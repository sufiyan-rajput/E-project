Table Fans

Table fans are compact, portable electric fans designed to sit on tables or desks.They provide targeted airflow, making them ideal for personal use in homes or offices.Most have adjustable speed settings, oscillation features,and tilting heads to direct air where needed.

Exaust Fans

Exuast fans are electrical devices mounted on ceilings to circulate air within a room,providing a cooling effect.They help maintain comfortable indoor temperatures,improve air circulation,and are energy-efficient alternatives to air conditioning systems.
